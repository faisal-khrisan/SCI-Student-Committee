About Us

The SCI Students' Committee at Albukhary International University (AIU) is a student-led body dedicated to fostering academic excellence, leadership, and innovation in the School of Computing and Informatics (SCI).

Our Mission

  • Foster collaboration through events
  • Promote ethical leadership
  • Enhance student experience

Our Vision

To be a leading student body driving innovation and positive change in computing and informatics.
